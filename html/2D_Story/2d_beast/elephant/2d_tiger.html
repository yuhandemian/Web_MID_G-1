The Bengal tiger is a subspecies of tiger found primarily in India, known for its power and beauty.
This tiger is one of the largest cat species in the world, typically growing up to 3 meters in length and weighing up to 300 kilograms.
Bengal tigers have distinctive stripes that are unique to each individual.
Their diet mainly consists of deer, wild boar, and small mammals.
