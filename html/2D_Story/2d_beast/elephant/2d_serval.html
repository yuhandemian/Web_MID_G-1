The serval is a medium-sized wild cat native to Africa, known for its long legs and large ears.
Servals typically grow up to 1 meter in length and weigh up to 18 kilograms. They primarily live in grasslands and use their excellent hearing to hunt small mammals, birds, and insects.
Servals have an impressive jumping ability, allowing them to catch prey in high places.
