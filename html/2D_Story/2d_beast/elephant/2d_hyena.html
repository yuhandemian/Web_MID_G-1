The spotted hyena is a large carnivorous mammal native to sub-Saharan Africa, known for its social structure and laughing calls.
Spotted hyenas typically grow up to 1.8 meters in length and weigh up to 80 kilograms. They live in groups and share the meat of their hunted prey.
Spotted hyenas are excellent hunters and also play a crucial role in scavenging carcasses.
